Rooted in the Neighborhood

We opened our doors in 2018 with a simple mission: serve coffee that respects the farmer, the roaster, and the drinker. Every bean is ethically sourced, every pastry is made from scratch, and every customer is treated like a regular.

No corporate playbook. No frozen dough. Just a small team that wakes up at 4 AM because we genuinely love what we do.

From Farm to Cup

Our Process

Great coffee doesn't happen by accident. Here's how we make every cup count.

01

Sourcing

We partner directly with farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala. Fair prices, sustainable practices.

02

Roasting

Small batches roasted in our back-room roaster. We profile every bean to find its sweet spot.

03

Brewing

Precise grind, exact temperature, proper extraction. Our baristas are trained, not just hired.

04

Serving

Every cup is tasted before it reaches you. If it's not perfect, we make it again. No exceptions.
